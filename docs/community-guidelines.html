Community Guidelines  ·  Last updated: March 2026

bingoo is a private, invite-based social game. Even in a private session, players must treat each other respectfully. These rules apply to nicknames, predictions, reactions, and any other user-generated content inside the app.

1. Be respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or intimidation
  • No hateful or discriminatory content
  • No sexual or explicit content
  • No doxxing, blackmail, or sharing private or sensitive information
  • No spam, repeated abuse, or disruptive behavior

2. Keep predictions playful

Predictions should stay lighthearted and game-appropriate. Do not use bingoo to shame, target, humiliate, or pressure another person.

3. Reporting

You can report a prediction or player directly in the app. Reports are reviewed manually. We may remove content, restrict future participation, or take other action when needed.
